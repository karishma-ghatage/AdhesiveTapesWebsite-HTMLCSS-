WE STICK WITH TRUST

Miracle Adhesive Tape is a global leader in the tape industry. For years, Miracle has provided customers the highest quality pressure sensitive adhesive tapes and related products.

Our Company converts and fabricates a wide variety of high quality pressure sensitive adhesive tapes and non-adhesive materials. The unique combinations of our adhesive products, dispensing equipment and technical services allow us to provide our customers with innovative solutions to their design, production, and packaging and purchasing requirements.

Whenever materials need to sealed, spliced, laminated, bonded, fastened, masked, protected or identified, you will find a solution with Miracle.

MARKETS SERVED

CONSTRUCTION

Miracle supplies a variety of construction products including products for window and door flashing, carpet seaming, tile adhesion, and window and door manufacturing.A comprehensive portfolio of Cloth, PVC, Polythene, Foil, Masking and Double Sided products for indoor and outdoor applications have been developed for every trade throughout the construction or repair process.

ELECTRICAL

As a 3M preferred specialty tape converter, Miracle is highly proficient in producing prototypes and fabricating electronic component parts for this fast growing high tech industry. From high temperature films such as polyimide to electrically conductive, thermally conductive or non-conductive adhesive tapes & insulating materials, Miracle continues to supply new and innovative materials used in the assembly and packaging of appliances and electronic products.

GRAPHICS AND PAINTING

Miracle offers adhesive tapes for graphics, small and large format displays, POP (Point of Purchase), stand up and all other advertising displays. Display graphic materials often used include bonding tapes, protective tapes, mounting and laminating tapes & adhesives. Miracle has standard sizes and specialty shapes for quick shipment, specializing in off the shelf POP bonding tapes.

PACKAGING

Miracle packing tapes are some of the most commonly used tapes in shipping and inventory management today, and with good reason. The special molecular structure and the resin stabilization of polypropylene offer excellent mechanical and optical properties, which are especially useful packaging.
